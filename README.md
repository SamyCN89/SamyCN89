# Hi, I'm Samy Castro
![banner](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/SamyCN89/SamyCN89/main/banner.png)

**Computational Neuroscientist**

I develop tools and analyses at the intersection of neuroscience, theory, and complex systems. I'm focusing my work on how brain dynamics emerge from a complex interactions shape by structural constraints.

---

## 🔬 Research

I'm a senior postdoctoral researcher at the **FunSy team** ([LNCA](https://lnca.unistra.fr), University of Strasbourg), working with Dr Demian Battaglia at the crossroads of neuroimaging, network theory, and theoretical modeling of the brain.

**Core topics:**
- Dynamic functional connectivity (dFC) and its speed/flexibility
- Metaconnectivity (MC): second-order statistics of brain state transitions
- Connectome-driven ignition and mesoscale brain dynamics
- Mouse & human neuroimaging
